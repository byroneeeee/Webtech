Arthur Neri

Arthur Madrigalejos Nery (born January 28, 1996) is a Filipino singer and songwriter. He signed a record deal in 2019 with Kean Cipriano 's label O/C under Viva Records, and released his debut album Letters Never Sent in the same year. He became well known in 2021 for his hit single "Pagsamo".

Adie

Favorite Band

Ben&Ben

Ben&Ben (formerly known as The Benjamins) are a folk pop band in the Philippines. Formed in 2015, the band have since been known for their popular hits that include "Pagtingin" and "Lifetime", among others.

Panic! at the disco

Panic! at the Disco is a solo project of American musician Brendon Urie. It was originally a pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2004 by childhood friends Urie, Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith, and Brent Wilson. They recorded their first demos while they were in high school.
